Our Menu

Chicken

Our signature chicken dishes are prepared with the finest free-range poultry, marinated in a secret blend of herbs and spices that have been passed down through generations. Each piece is perfectly grilled to achieve a golden, crispy exterior while maintaining juicy, tender meat inside. Whether you prefer it grilled, roasted, or simmered in a rich, aromatic sauce, our chicken dishes promise a burst of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. We source our chicken from local farms that practice ethical and sustainable farming methods, ensuring not only great taste but also peace of mind. Served with a side of seasonal vegetables and your choice of rice or mashed potatoes, our chicken dishes are the perfect comfort food for any occasion.

For those who love a little heat, try our spicy chili chicken, featuring a fiery blend of crushed red peppers, garlic, and ginger that will awaken your senses. If you prefer something milder, our classic lemon herb chicken is a timeless favorite that never fails to impress. Each dish is prepared fresh to order, ensuring that every bite is as flavorful as the first. Whether you're dining in with family or ordering for a special celebration, our chicken dishes are sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Beef

Experience the rich, savory goodness of our premium beef selections, sourced from grass-fed cattle raised in pristine pastures. Our beef is aged to perfection, ensuring maximum tenderness and depth of flavor in every bite. From succulent steaks to hearty stews, our beef dishes are crafted with care and passion by our expert chefs. The star of our menu is the classic grilled sirloin, seasoned simply with sea salt and cracked black pepper to let the natural flavors shine through. For those seeking something more indulgent, our slow-braised beef short ribs, cooked for hours in a rich red wine sauce, melt in your mouth and leave you craving more.

We take pride in offering a variety of beef preparations that cater to every palate. Our spicy beef stir-fry, featuring crisp bell peppers and onions in a savory soy-ginger glaze, is a customer favorite among those who enjoy Asian-inspired flavors. If you're in the mood for something hearty and comforting, our classic beef stew with root vegetables is the perfect choice. Each dish is accompanied by carefully selected sides that complement the robust flavors of the beef. Whether you're a steak connoisseur or simply looking for a satisfying meal, our beef dishes are guaranteed to impress.

Sushi

Immerse yourself in the art of Japanese cuisine with our exquisite sushi creations, prepared with the freshest ingredients and meticulous attention to detail. Our sushi chefs are masters of their craft, trained in the traditional techniques of Edomae-style sushi. From delicate nigiri to creative maki rolls, each piece is a work of art that delights both the eyes and the palate. We source our fish daily from the best suppliers, ensuring that only the highest quality seafood, from buttery salmon to rich tuna, makes it to your plate. Our signature rolls, like the spicy tuna crunch and the dragon roll, combine vibrant flavors and textures that will transport you straight to Tokyo.

Vegetarian options are also available, featuring fresh avocado, cucumber, and pickled vegetables rolled in perfectly seasoned sushi rice and wrapped in crispy nori. Our sushi platters are ideal for sharing with friends and family, offering a variety of flavors that cater to all tastes. Whether you're a sushi aficionado or trying it for the first time, our chefs are happy to recommend dishes that suit your preferences. Served with wasabi, pickled ginger, and premium soy sauce, our sushi is a celebration of Japanese culinary traditions that will leave you wanting more.
